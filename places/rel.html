Masjid Jabal Arafah
Masjid Jabal Arafah in Batam was designed from the outset as a modern, multi-functional mosque. It is located in the central business district of Batam City and covers an area of ​​4,607 square meters .
17.7 km from Hang Nadim International Airport
3.5 km from Harbour Bay Ferry Terminal
Free entry
The mosque is open for 24 hours.
Why should you visit?
MJA observation tower
Food & souvenirs stalls available
Library provided
Located near shopping center
Visitor must walk up stair to reach the praying hall

Masjid Tanwirun Naja
Masjid Tanwirun Naja/Masjid Tanjak does not have a dome like normal mosques, the shape of the Tanjak Mosque resembles the Malay tanjak (a typical Malay head covering that is pointed upwards). Tanjak Mosque can accommodate 1,250 worshipers
0.6 km from Hang Nadim International Airport
11.6 km from Batam Center Ferry Terminal
Free entry
The mosque is open for 24 hours.
Why should you visit?
Iconic architecture
Located in the airport area
Surrounded by grass field
You should visit this before going to the airport.

Address:

Patung Rajawali, Batu Besar, Kecamatan Nongsa, Kota Batam, Kepulauan Riau

Maha Vihara Duta Maitreya

Address:

Komplek Maha Vihara Duta Maitreya Bukit Beruntung, Sungai Panas, Kec. Batam Kota, Kota Batam, Kepulauan Riau
Maha Vihara Duta Maitreya was established in 1991 on four hectares of land . In 1999, it was inaugurated. To this day, Maha Vihara Duta Maitreya remains an active place of worship. The temple could accommodate at least 6000 people.
13.2 km from Hang Nadim International Airport
3.0 km from Batam Center Ferry Terminal
Free entry
07:00 - 17:00 (GMT+7)
Why should you visit?
Biggest in Indonesia
Vegan food & souvenirs stalls available
Three wide praying hall
There’s so many statues in the complex

Vihara Budi Bhakti & Kelenteng Tua Pek Kong

Address:

Jl. Pembangunan, Lubuk Baja Kota, Kec. Lubuk Baja, Kota Batam, Kepulauan Riau

Vihara Budhi Bhakti, or Tua Pek Kong Bio, is a place of worship for Tri Dharma Buddhists. It was built in 1970 , and the complex consists of two main buildings: a temple at the front and a monastery building slightly inward.
17.4 km from Hang Nadim International Airport
3.9 km from Harbour Bay Ferry Terminal
Free entry
11:00 - 22:00 (GMT+7)
Why should you visit?
Oldest in Batam
Vegan food & souvenirs stalls available
Two religion worship places in one complex
There are many rituals during Chinese New Year
