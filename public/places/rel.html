Masjid Tanwirun Naja

Masjid Tanwirun Naja/Masjid Tanjak does not have a dome like normal mosques, the shape of the Tanjak Mosque resembles the Malay tanjak (a typical Malay head covering that is pointed upwards). Tanjak Mosque can accommodate 1,250 worshipers

11.6 km   from  Batam  Center  Ferry  Terminal

Masjid Sultan Mahmud Riayat Syah

The Sultan Mahmud Riayat Syah Mosque, has a 99-meter-high minaret with Malay and Arabic architectural styles. It also features eight 17-meter-high membrane umbrellas, similar to those found at the Prophet's Mosque. The total area of ​​the Sultan Mahmud Riayat Syah Mosque is 5.7114 hectares .

28.4 km from  Hang  Nadim  International  Airport

15.7 km   from  Sekupang  Ferry  Terminal

Free entry

The mosque is open for 24 hours.

Why should you visit?

Has a very spacious area

Located in Tanjunguncang industrial area

Have many umbrellas

Many often jogged around the mosque

Address:
 Tj. Uncang, Kec. Batu Aji, Kota Batam, Kepulauan Riau
