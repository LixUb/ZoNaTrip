Gemilang Budaya Melayu

Berjejak di Warisan, Bertapak di Masa Depan

What Awaits You

Gemilang Budaya Melayu is an annual cultural festival held by ZoNa dedicated to celebrating the rich heritage and vibrant traditions of the Melayu community. The festival brings together art, music, dance, culinary delights, and traditional crafts to showcase the timeless beauty of Melayu culture.

The global theme, “Berjejak di Warisan, Bertapak di Masa Depan” (Rooted in Heritage, Stepping into the Future), highlights the importance of preserving cultural roots while embracing innovation and diversity in a modern world.
